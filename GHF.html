One Boot in the Grave

Description

Gotta Have Faith is a 2.5D platformer bullet hell.

Project Info

This project was made with a group of 7 People; 1 Programmer, 2 Designers, 1 Audio, 3 Artists. This project had to be finished within 3 weeks

My Contributions

I was the primary programmer for this assesment, i also designed the 1st level, and was the gameplay designer/balancer. Because of the size of this team, I took the mantle of team leader. I assigned jobs to the team, held standups daily, and checked in with everyone seeing if they needed more resources or time. I Managed when people where late and found soulutions solving that. Because i was not the sole designer, i had to make my dev tools easy to use for non programmers. this was a challenge for me, as i allways set up my dev tools to make sense to the person who wrote that program. however for this i had to think from the prespective of a designer to ensure that the tools where easy to use. While the outcome was not great, i got a whole lot of experience and knoledge on making effective dev tools.
