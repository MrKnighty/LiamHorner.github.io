Featured Games

LOG

LOG is a survival horror crafter. You are tasked with the objective of survival, and building your LOG cabin. You start in a forest with absolutely nothing. However, the forest is not what it seems. when you go to chop down a tree, you might find that it is a sentient tree! during night time you will not be able to sleep, as you are under consant threat by the unwelcoming forest. your only chance of survival is to build that cabin

One Boot in The Grave

One Boot in the Grave is a 2d roguelite inspired by Vampire Surivors. You have to defeat increasingly difficult waves of enemies, each enemy will drop a boot. As you collect more boots you will gain power and will be able to Upgrade, or find new spells. As you progress, you can start making up your own builds.

Gotta Have Faith

Gotta Have Faith is a 2.5D platformer bullet hell game with minor roguelite elements. Your goal is to reach the end boss in every stage. However, you cannot damage any enemies directly. To defeat the bosses, you must dodge every attack they throw at you since the bosses take damage from casting spells at you. If you get hit by a spell, you give them a lot of health back.
