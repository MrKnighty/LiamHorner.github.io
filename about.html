Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Liam Horner, and I am a game programmer. I am currently in my third year of university, studying for a Bachelor of Software Engineering with a focus on Game Programming. My initial drive for game development stemmed from my love for games. While I didn't start with a specific goal, I discovered my passion for programming during my Certificate III course. I have skills in Unity, C#, C++, a bit of OpenGL, Unreal Engine 5, development for PS5, and starter knowledge in Godot. I also have beginner knowledge in data mining and machine learning. After graduation, I plan to join a AAA game company to sharpen and hone my skills for at least five years. Eventually, I aspire to either start my own indie company or join an existing one to further pursue my creative ambitions.

Outside of game development, I love cooking! I take a chaotic approach when cooking, choosing to mostly ignore recipes to experiment with what works and what does not. While this mostly ends up poorly, I occasionally come up with some really good dishes!
